Welcome to Quizzes

Mental health quizzes can be valuable tools for self-assessment and understanding potential mental health issues. However, they are not diagnostic tools. If you or someone you know is experiencing significant mental health concerns, it is important to seek professional help from a licensed mental health provider.

Depression Screening Quiz

1. Over the past two weeks, how often have you felt down, depressed, or hopeless?

2. How often have you lost interest or pleasure in activities you used to enjoy?

3. How often do you feel tired or have little energy?

Anxiety Assessment Quiz

1. How often do you experience excessive worry about different areas of your life (e.g., work, school, relationships)?

2. How often do you find it difficult to control your worry?

3. Do you experience physical symptoms like a racing heart, sweating, or shaking when you're anxious?

Stress Level Quiz

1. How often do you feel overwhelmed by the demands of daily life?

2. How often do you experience physical symptoms such as headaches, muscle tension, or stomach issues due to stress?

3. How often do you find it difficult to relax or calm your mind?

Bipolar Disorder Screening Quiz

1. Have you ever experienced periods of extremely high energy, euphoria, or irritability?

2. Have you experienced periods where you need less sleep than usual and still feel energetic?

3. Have you had episodes of depression where you felt sad, hopeless, or disinterested in activities you usually enjoy?

Insomnia Quiz

1. How often do you have difficulty falling asleep?

2. How often do you wake up during the night and have trouble getting back to sleep?

3. How often do you wake up feeling unrefreshed?

Eating Disorder Assessment Quiz

1. How often do you feel out of control when eating?

2. How often do you avoid eating in public due to fear of judgment?

3. How often do you feel guilty or ashamed after eating?

Children's Mental Health Quiz

1. How often does your child seem withdrawn or uninterested in social interactions?

2. How often does your child exhibit excessive worry or anxiety?

3. How often does your child have difficulty concentrating or focusing on tasks?

Postpartum Depression Quiz

1. How often do you feel overwhelmed or unable to cope with caring for your baby?

2. How often do you feel sad or cry for no apparent reason?

3. How often do you have trouble sleeping, even when your baby is asleep?
