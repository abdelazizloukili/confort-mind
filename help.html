Domestic & Sexual Violence

A toll-free number to help women victims of violence

080008888

This toll-free number provides users with access to advice and guidance services. It is supported by numerous government entities, including the Ministry of Justice, Ministry of Health, the Directorate General of National Security, the Royal Gendarmerie, and the National Mutual Aid Institution.

Abdelmalek Essaâdi University in Tetouan - toll-free number

0802081111

This toll-free number allows individuals to report acts of sexual harassment and gender-based violence. To help prevent psychological difficulties resulting from these incidents, a psychologist will be available to assist those affected.

Mental Health Condition and Famliy Support

Fondation Orient-Occident psychological assistance toll-free number

0800004146

The Fondation Orient-Occident has introduced a toll-free number to offer psychological support to migrants, refugees, and asylum seekers in Morocco. This service is available every Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday from 9 a.m. to 1 p.m.

listening center - Casablanca-Settat

0639099551

The National Order of Physicians has established regional offices across the Kingdom with telephone numbers dedicated to citizens. These listening centers are focused on providing psychological support and monitoring for individuals in need, available from 2 p.m. to 4 p.m., Monday to Thursday.

The service operates from 12 pm to 2 pm, offering channels for listening, support, and psychological counseling. It helps individuals overcome suffering, cope with difficulties, and manage psychological pressures. The service provides various types of guidance and counseling for conditions such as stress, panic, fears, and obsessive-compulsive disorder, especially related to virus infection, home quarantine, and preventive measures. For consultation and communication via WhatsApp, and to send text or voice messages, please contact the numbers provided. Services are available Monday to Saturday.
